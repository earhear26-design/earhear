Audiology Services

PTA (Pure Tone Audiometry)

Comprehensive hearing assessment to determine the softest sounds you can hear at different frequencies.

Impedance Audiometry

Tests middle ear function and identifies issues like fluid buildup or eardrum problems.

OAE (Otoacoustic Emissions)

Quick screening test to check inner ear function, commonly used for newborn hearing screening.

BERA (Brainstem Evoked Response Audiometry)

Advanced test to evaluate hearing pathway from ear to brain, ideal for infants and difficult-to-test patients.

Hearing Aid Fitting

Professional fitting and customization of hearing aids tailored to your specific hearing loss and lifestyle needs.

Hearing Aid Programming

Advanced programming and fine-tuning of digital hearing aids for optimal performance.

Hearing Aid Repair & Accessories

Complete repair services and accessories including batteries, cleaning tools, and replacement parts.

Cochlear Implants

Consultation and support for cochlear implant candidates with severe to profound hearing loss.

Cochlear Implant Mapping

Professional programming and adjustment of cochlear implant processors for optimal hearing.

Cochlear Implant Accessories & Parts

Complete range of cochlear implant accessories, replacement parts, and maintenance supplies.
